# Melodyx Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 11, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Melodyx ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Melodyx: Daily Melody Puzzle & Music Learning (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: If you create an account, we collect your display name, email address, and password.
- **Profile Data**: Optional profile photo, bio, and music preferences.
- **User Content**: Puzzles you create, playlist names, and shared content.
- **Communications**: Messages you send to our support team.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Gameplay Data**: Puzzle attempts, scores, streaks, achievements, and game statistics.
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns.
- **Performance Data**: Crash logs, error reports, and app performance metrics.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Social Logins**: If you sign in with Apple, Google, or social accounts, we receive basic profile information.
- **Payment Processors**: Purchase confirmation from Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the App
- Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
- Display leaderboards and enable multiplayer features
- Send notifications about daily puzzles and events (with your consent)
- Personalize your gaming experience with AI-powered recommendations
- Calculate eco-points and carbon offset contributions
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Respond to support requests and feedback
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do NOT

- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your information with advertisers for targeting
- Track you across other apps or websites
- Use your data for purposes unrelated to the App

### 4.2 We May Share Data With

- **Service Providers**: Firebase (analytics, authentication, database), RevenueCat (purchases), Sentry (crash reporting)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly agree to sharing

### 4.3 Aggregated Data

We may share anonymous, aggregated statistics about gameplay patterns for research or marketing purposes. This data cannot identify individual users.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Storage

- **Local Storage**: Game progress, preferences, and cached data are stored on your device
- **Cloud Storage**: Account data, leaderboards, and multiplayer data are stored on Firebase servers in the United States
- **Retention**: We retain data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services

### 5.2 Security Measures

- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Access controls and authentication for all systems
- Secure coding practices and code reviews

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Portability

You can request a copy of your data at any time by contacting privacy@melodyx.app.

### 6.2 Correction

You can update your account information within the App settings.

### 6.3 Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from App Settings > Delete Account. This action is irreversible.

### 6.4 Opt-Out Options

- **Notifications**: Disable in device settings or App settings
- **Analytics**: Toggle off in App Settings > Privacy
- **Personalization**: Disable AI recommendations in Settings

## 7. Children's Privacy

Melodyx is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction) without verifiable parental consent.

If you believe we have collected information from a child without proper consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@melodyx.app.

## 8. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## 9. Compliance

European users have additional rights including:
- Right to access, rectification, and erasure
- Right to restrict or object to processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

**Legal Basis**: We process data based on:
- Contract performance (providing the App)
- Legitimate interests (improving services, security)
- Consent (marketing communications)

### 9.2 CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected

- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise CCPA rights, contact privacy@melodyx.app.

### 9.3 Other Jurisdictions

We comply with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where the App is available.
