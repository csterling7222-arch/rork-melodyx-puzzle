# Melodyx Audio Content Legal Notice

## Fair Use Policy

Melodyx uses short audio excerpts (<30 seconds) for educational and transformative purposes under fair use principles. These snippets are intended to help users learn music recognition and appreciation.

### Educational Purpose

All audio content in Melodyx serves an educational purpose:
- Teaching music recognition through interactive gameplay
- Helping users identify melodies, rhythms, and musical patterns
- Encouraging music discovery and artist appreciation
- Providing learning tools for music education

### Transformative Use

The use of audio snippets in Melodyx is transformative because:
- Snippets are used in an interactive game context, not passive listening
- The purpose is educational (music learning) rather than entertainment substitution
- Users are encouraged to stream full tracks on official platforms
- The experience creates new value through gamification and learning

## Content Sources

### MIDI Recreations
Most audio content consists of MIDI recreations of melodies:
- Not original recordings
- Created specifically for educational use
- Focus on melodic patterns for recognition

### Public Domain
Classical compositions and traditional melodies:
- Works where copyright has expired
- Traditional folk music
- Public domain arrangements

### Licensed Content (Premium)
For premium subscribers:
- Content licensed through services like Epidemic Sound
- Properly attributed and compensated
- Used under appropriate licensing terms

## User Responsibilities

Users agree to:
